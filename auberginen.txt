ingredients:
  - 3 Eggplant
  - 100-200 ml olive oil
  - 3 cloves of garlic
  - 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  - Salt + Pepper
  - Italian herbs

instructions:
   Aubergine into quarters, then cut into 1.5-3 cm strips
 
   Finely chop garlic.
 
   The oil with the chopped garlic in saucepan and fry the eggplant.

   With the chopped tomatoes (and possibly some water or wine) Delete.

   Season with salt, pepper and Italian herbs.
